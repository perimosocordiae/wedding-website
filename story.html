How We Met: CJ's Story

It was my first year of graduate school in Amherst, Massachusetts, and I was busy trying to get settled: adjusting to grad student life, learning the area, and making lots of new friends. I quickly found that the grad student community in Amherst was pretty small, which means that you'll probably bump into people without even trying. Never was that more apparent than with Angela, when we realized that we happened to be living in the same apartment complex, only a few doors away!

Realizing that I'd been given several chances and still not managed to ask her out, I did what any reasonable guy would do&em; I invited myself to her ski trip in Killington, Vermont.

To my surprise (and relief), she was happy to have me along, and that snowy day of skiing set in motion a series of dates that led to our beautiful relationship. We've come a long way since those cold Massachusetts days, and I'm incredibly excited for our future together.

How We Met: Angela's Story

I first got introduced to CJ in graduate school by my friends in the psychology department. (Shout out to Tessa Lundquist!) We didn't meet again until we were both coincidentally at a bar in Amherst. I was there with Polymer Science kids having a few drinks after our holiday party, and CJ was there with a friend touring all the Amherst bars. He asked me to join them and I said yes! At the end of the night, CJ and I hopped on the bus to Sunderland and discovered that I lived two doors down from him.

After that, we only spoke once or twice while home for the holidays. Back at school, we went on a few ski trips together and I realized I started to like him. After those ski trips we started to date, then fall in love.

Since then, we've been on many adventures together throughout the world. We faced all the hurdles of graduate school together and all of life's ups and downs. We even moved to Texas! I have been forever happy since I met CJ and he has made my life a million times better. I cannot wait to marry him and spend the rest of my life with my best friend!
