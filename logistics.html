Where to Go

Our wedding will be in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The ceremony and the reception venues are located in the historic Oakland and Shadyside neighborhoods, conveniently close to beautiful university campuses, expansive parks, and the heart of the city.

The ceremony will take place in the University of Pittsburgh's Heinz Memorial Chapel, 4200 Fifth Avenue.

The reception will be held at the Mansions on Fifth hotel, 5105 Fifth Avenue.

When to Arrive

The ceremony will begin on Saturday, November 12th at 1:30 pm. Please plan to arrive early!

After the ceremony, you'll have one hour to make your way to the reception venue at Mansions on Fifth. Transportation will by provided by the Wyndham Hotel.

The reception will begin with a cocktail hour at 4 pm, and the party will continue until 9 pm.

Accomodations

We've reserved a room block at the Wyndham Pittsburgh University Center, which is conveniently located near both the ceremony and reception venues. To book in our block, use this link or manually input group code 11116841CC on the hotel website.

Rooms are also available at Mansions on Fifth.

Parking

Parking for Heinz Chapel is limited, with street parking along S. Bellefield Ave and surrounding roads.

We will provide complimentary valet parking for the reception at Mansions on Fifth.

Air Travel

The best airport to use is Pittsburgh International Airport (PIT). It takes about 30 minutes to drive from the airport to the city without traffic, and public transportation options are limited. Please let us know if you need help arranging rides.
